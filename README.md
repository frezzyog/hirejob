**HireAI** is designed to bridge the gap between talented job seekers and top employers in Cambodia. By leveraging artificial intelligence, we move beyond traditional keyword matching to understand the *context* of a candidate's skills and a company's needs.

Our mission is to:
1.  **Simplify Recruitment**: Automate sourcing and screening for employers.
2.  **Empower Candidates**: Provide tools like AI Interview Prep and Resume Builders to help candidates put their best foot forward.
3.  **Localize Opportunities**: Focus on the growing Cambodian tech and business sectors (ABA Bank, Wing, etc.).
